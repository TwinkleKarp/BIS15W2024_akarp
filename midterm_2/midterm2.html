  1. (1 point) Notice that there are some incidents identified as “NOT COUNTED”. These should be removed from the data because they were either not sharks, unverified, or were provoked. It’s OK to replace the sharks object.
sharks_new <- sharks %>% 
  filter(incident_num!="NOT COUNTED")
